Common questions

Why do I need a lawyer for my DUI case?

Online information is not a substitute for counsel with deep experience in Pennsylvania DUI law, procedure, and local practice. A lawyer can protect your rights at every stage. If you are not sure, schedule a consultation so your situation can be reviewed in context.

Can I refuse the breath test?

Pennsylvania has implied consent rules tied to driver licensing. Refusals and testing choices can have serious license consequences. What is right for you depends on the exact facts. Discuss the sequence in your case with an attorney as early as possible. Read more in the full FAQ.

What are the different levels of DUI?

Pennsylvania commonly uses three general impairment tiers based on BAC, with additional issues when drugs or refusals are present. See the penalty overview.
